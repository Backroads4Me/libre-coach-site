The best way to get in touch is through our community spaces.

### Technical Support & Discussion
For questions about building your own LibreCoach or troubleshooting your setup, please join us on the **[LibreCoach Community Forum](https://forum.librecoach.com)**.

### Bug Reports & Contributions
If you've found a bug or want to contribute to the code, please visit our **[GitHub Repository](https://github.com/Backroads4Me)**.
